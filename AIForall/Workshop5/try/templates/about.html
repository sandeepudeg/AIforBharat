About Pune

Pune is a city in Maharashtra, India, known for its rich cultural heritage, educational institutions, and thriving IT industry. The city has played a significant role in India's independence movement and continues to be a center of culture, education, and innovation.

From the historic Peths with their traditional Marathi culture to modern IT hubs like Hinjewadi, Pune offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. The city is famous for its food, festivals, temples, and vibrant lifestyle.
